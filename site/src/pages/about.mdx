# About Learn With Jason

_Learn With Jason_ is a live show featuring [Jason Lengstorf](https://lengstorf.com) and experts from around the web community.

On the show, Jason and his guest pair up to build something together. This is often some kind of web development project, but really anything goes as long as thereâ€™s something to learn.

The show usually airs every Monday and Thursday [on Twitch](https://twitch.tv/jlengstorf), with occasional special episodes at different times. Check [the schedule](/schedule) for details.

All episodes are cross-posted [to YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLz8Iz-Fnk_eTpvd49Sa77NiF8Uqq5Iykx) a day or so after they air.

## About Jason Lengstorf

Jason Lengstorf is a developer, community advocate, and professional goofball who loves learning new things and sharing them with the community. In addition to _Learn With Jason_, he also creates [online courses](https://egghead.io/instructors/jason-lengstorf) and [workshops](https://frontendmasters.com/teachers/jason-lengstorf/), [writes about things](https://lengstorf.com), and [spreads love on Twitter](https://twitter.com/jlengstorf).
